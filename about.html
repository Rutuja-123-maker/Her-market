About Us

We are a women-led Self-Help Group (SHG) focused on empowering women in rural and urban communities by providing them with the tools, knowledge, and opportunities to become financially independent. Our SHG consists of talented women producing homemade goods, ranging from snacks like fryums and pickles to handcrafted decor and jewelry.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower women to create a sustainable livelihood through entrepreneurship, skill-building, and access to direct markets. We strive to build a platform where women can showcase their products and talents, reaching customers across the country.

Our Vision

We envision a future where women, regardless of their background, have the opportunity to be financially independent and contribute to the growth of their communities. By creating a marketplace for their handmade products, we aim to support economic growth at the grassroots level.

What We Do

  • Support women in setting up small businesses.
  • Provide training on product development and quality improvement.
  • Offer digital and financial literacy to help women manage their finances better.
  • Connect women to direct markets through our online platform.
