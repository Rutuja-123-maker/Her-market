Join Our Women's Self-Help Group (SHG)

Self-Help Groups (SHGs) in India are a powerful tool in promoting women's empowerment, economic independence, and social transformation. By joining an SHG, women gain financial freedom through collective efforts, skill development, and entrepreneurship.

Why Join an SHG?

  • Empower yourself with financial independence.
  • Enhance your skills in manufacturing, handicrafts, and business.
  • Sell your homemade products like pickles, fryums, spices, and more.
  • Be part of a supportive and encouraging community.
  • Get access to training, resources, and market opportunities.

How to Join

Fill out the registration form below and become a member of our thriving SHG community. Share your talents, skills, and products with the world!

Our SHG Success Stories

Savita's Story

Savita, a mother of two, joined the SHG in her village two years ago. Through the SHG, she learned how to make and market homemade pickles. Today, Savita sells her products across multiple cities and earns a steady income.

Manjula's Story

Manjula started making handicrafts as a hobby. After joining the SHG, she received training on expanding her business. Today, her handicrafts are popular among customers, and she employs two other women from her village.
